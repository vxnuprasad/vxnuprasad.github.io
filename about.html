MY STORY

From curiosity to GreyNod Labs

Cybersecurity Researcher • Security Consultant • Security Tool Developer

My journey into cybersecurity began in 2019 after discovering the field through technology-focused content online. What started as simple curiosity quickly evolved into a passion for understanding how systems work, how networks communicate, and how security vulnerabilities can be identified and mitigated.

In the early days, I spent countless hours exploring Linux, experimenting with Kali Linux, learning command-line tools, and practicing on Android devices using Termux.

After completing my higher secondary education in Computer Science, I pursued a Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering.

Today, my primary focus is GreyNod Labs, a cybersecurity company dedicated to helping organizations identify, assess, and remediate security vulnerabilities before they can be exploited.
